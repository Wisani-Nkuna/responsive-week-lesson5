Tshwane Lifestyle (Meyerspark)

15 Bronkhorstspruit road , Pretoria, Gauteng, South Africa

Join in the vibrant festivities at Tshwane Lifestyle restaurant, a fan favourite among many. Enjoy dishes like Mogodu, beef or pork trotters, braai platters, tripe and more. They also serve beautiful sides of samp, home-made chakalaka and pap. The restaurant is open from Monday to Wednesday from 10am to 5pm and Thursday to Sunday from 12noon to 10pm.
